Step-by-Step Commands

Sit

1. Hold a treat close to your dog's nose.
2. Move your hand up, letting their head follow the treat and causing their bottom to lower.

Stay

1. Ask your dog to sit.
2. Open your palm and say “stay.”
3. Take a few steps back.

Come

1. Put a leash on your dog.
2. Say “come” in an inviting tone while gently pulling the leash.
3. Reward them when they come to you.
